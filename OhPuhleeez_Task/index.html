Event Details

oh Puhleeez Branding Agency recently conducted a pool campus placement drive at Arya College of Engineering and IT, Jaipur . The agency sought skilled individuals for diverse roles, including Front-end Web Developer, Back-end Developer, Business Analyst, Video Editor, and Graphic Designer. Students had the chance to showcase their talents for crafting user-friendly interfaces, developing backend logic, analyzing business processes, editing engaging videos, and creating visually stunning graphics. The event aimed to bring dynamic and creative minds to contribute to the agency's growth in the field of branding and design.

Date: January 20, 2024 - January 21, 2024

Time: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Venue: Arya College of Engineering and IT, Jaipur
